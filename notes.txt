<h2>Integer Multiplication</h2>

<h3>"Paper and Pencil" Example</h3>

<p>The multiplication example demonstrates multiplying two integers using a basic step-by-step method.</p><!-- END_SECTION -->

<ul>
    <li><strong>Multiplicand:</strong> 1000</li>
    <li><strong>Multiplier:</strong> 1001</li>
</ul><!-- END_SECTION -->

<h3>Step-by-Step Calculation</h3>

<ol>
    <li>
        Multiply 1000 by the least significant digit (rightmost) of the multiplier (1):
        <ul>
            <li>Result: 1000</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
        Shift left, multiply 1000 by the next digit of the multiplier (0):
        <ul>
            <li>Result: 0000</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
        Shift again, multiply 1000 by the third digit of the multiplier (0):
        <ul>
            <li>Result: 0000</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
        Shift once more, multiply 1000 by the most significant digit (leftmost) of the multiplier (1):
        <ul>
            <li>Result: 1000</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
</ol><!-- END_SECTION -->

<h3>Final Product</h3>

<p>The calculation yields the following intermediate results added together:</p>

<ul>
    <li>1000</li>
    <li>0000</li>
    <li>0000</li>
    <li>+ 1000</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Final Product:</strong> 01001000</p><!-- END_SECTION -->
